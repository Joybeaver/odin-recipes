Pumpkin puree recipe

Description

Easy-to-made pumpkin puree that is sweet and good for you!

This pumpkin puree doesn't have any sugar in it and tastes like an amazing dessert. Moreover, you can keep it in a fridge for a few days for a healthy snack!

Ingredients

  • A half of a pumpkin or a whole pumpkin
  • A few teaspoons of honey
  • 200ml of sugar-free apple juice
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Wash the pumpkin and cut it in half or quarters.
  2. Put it in the oven and raise the temperature to around 220 c.
  3. When the temperature reaches that point, turn the heat to medium.
  4. Bake for around an hour.
  5. Take the pumpkin out, let it cool down a bit, and cut off the peel.
  6. Cut the pumpkin in pieces that will fit into the blender.
  7. Pour around 100 ml of juice into the blender.
  8. Put in half of the pumpkin (or however much fits), add 1-2 teaspoons of honey and a pinch of salt. Blend it till it turns into puree.
  9. Eat it warm right away, or put it into jars and into the fridge (when it's cooled down).
  10. Repeat till there's no pumpkin left!
