Carrot cake

Description

An easy-to-made carrot cake that anyone can make.

I've done this in a multicooker, can you can surely do this in an oven

It's also delicious if you put salted pistachios on top, but be careful not to put any shells

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Cream
  • Carrots
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Salt
  • Baking powder
  • Baking soda

Steps

  1. Peel and grate the carrots
  2. Sieve the flour
  3. Mix flour, sugar, vanilla, salt, baking powder, and baking soda in one bowl
  4. Beat the eggs and combine with grated carrots
  5. Combine wet and dry ingredients and mix thoroughly
  6. Put the mass in the multicooker at 150 degrees baking program for 50 minutes
